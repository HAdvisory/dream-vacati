How This Tool Works

Each destination has a peak season window (best weather, ideal conditions), a shoulder season (acceptable weather, lower prices), and a period to avoid. The tool surfaces destinations where your selected month falls in the peak or shoulder window, filters by budget tier and trip style, and shows relevant planning links. It does not use AI or backend calls — all recommendations run locally in your browser from a curated dataset of 20+ destinations.

Budget tiers: Budget = $80–150/person/day on the ground; Mid-Range = $150–350/person/day; Luxury = $350+/person/day. Flights are not included in these estimates.
