Best Caribbean Family Resorts 2026: Where Kids Splash, Giggle & Explore (While You Relax)

By Dwight H. · Travel Writer · 12+ countries visited · Updated May 2026

Top resorts that actually get family life — built-in kid fun, optional nanny services, and room for parents to breathe.

Traveling with kids doesn't have to feel like a logistics mission—especially when the Caribbean is calling with sun-kissed beaches, clear water, and resorts designed for families. Here are our top picks with kid-approved perks and real nanny service pricing—because sometimes, mama needs a break too.

Traveling with a pet instead? Check out our guide to flying your dog to Europe—paperwork, costs, and all.

1) Beaches Resorts — Turks & Caicos & Jamaica

The vibe: If Disneyland had an ocean, this would be it. Water parks, character parades, and gourmet options for picky eaters.

  • Why kids love it: Waterslides, splash zones, Xbox lounges.
  • Parent perk: Nanny services included with some suite levels; add-on ~$20–$25/hr.
  • Pro tip: Book concierge or butler-level rooms for included nanny hours.

2) Franklyn D. Resort — Runaway Bay, Jamaica

The vibe: Cozy, authentic, and famous for your own dedicated vacation nanny (included in the rate).

  • Why kids love it: Crafts, cooking classes, boat rides, nonstop attention.
  • Parent perk: Feels like you brought your favorite babysitter. Nanny is assigned on arrival and stays with your family the entire trip.
  • Good to know: Tipping is customary—~$20/day.

How to book this resort →

3) Windjammer Landing — St. Lucia

The vibe: Villa-style living with kitchens and plunge pools; privacy on tap. Great for families who want resort perks but condo-style space.

  • Why kids love it: Beach games, snorkeling, banana boats.
  • Parent perk: Nanny services available on demand (~$10–$15/hr).

How to book this resort →

4) Atlantis Paradise Island — Bahamas

The vibe: 141-acre water park, dolphin interactions, and a massive marine habitat. This is the big-budget spectacle option.

  • Why kids love it: Slides, lazy rivers, sea creatures galore.
  • Parent heads-up: Incredible—but extras add up. Nanny ~$25–$35/hr.

How to book this resort →

5) Coconut Bay Beach Resort — St. Lucia

The vibe: Split sides—Splash for families and Harmony for adults. Kidz Klub accepts little ones from 3 months.

  • Why kids love it: Water parks, petting zoo, scavenger hunts.
  • Parent perk: Babysitting roughly ~$10–$12/hr for infants/toddlers.
