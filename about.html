About DreamVacati

A travel planning utility — not a booking site, not an influencer feed. Destination data, honest cost breakdowns, and free tools built for travelers who want to plan well, not just get inspired.

Our Mission

DreamVacati exists because most travel content answers the wrong question. "Greece is beautiful" tells you nothing useful about whether June or September is the right month for your budget, your tolerance for crowds, or the experience you actually want. Most destination guides stop at inspiration and leave the real decision-making work to you.

This platform is built around one question: what do you actually need to know to make a good travel decision? Every destination guide starts with timing, real costs, and honest trade-offs. Every budget breakdown uses actual numbers — not ranges so wide they're meaningless.

The free planning tools fill gaps that most travel research leaves open: a budget calculator built on the 40/25/20/15 framework, a packing checklist that adapts to trip type and climate, a pet travel cost estimator based on current airline fees and USDA requirements, and an entry requirements guide across 15 destinations. No account required, no upsell.

Why DreamVacati Exists

DreamVacati was created to help travelers plan smarter trips using practical travel research, destination comparisons, budgeting tools, and real-world travel logistics.

The platform focuses on helping travelers avoid common planning mistakes, compare destinations more effectively, understand realistic travel costs, and make more informed booking decisions.

Every guide, tool, and recommendation is designed to prioritize clarity, usability, and honest travel planning over hype-driven tourism content.

Built for travelers who want practical planning tools, not just travel inspiration.

How We Research and Write

Real cost data
Budget estimates come from current booking platforms, airline fee schedules, and verified travel research — not ballpark ranges. Where data ages, we update it.
No paid rankings
No destination, hotel, or service pays to appear in our guides or tool results. Editorial recommendations reflect usefulness to the traveler, not commission rates.
Official sources for requirements data
Entry requirements, visa rules, and health documentation in our tools are cross-referenced with official government and embassy sources. Dated and linked where possible.
Utility over volume
We publish fewer, more useful guides rather than thin articles optimized for search volume. Each piece is meant to answer a real planning question, not just rank for a keyword.
