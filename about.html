About DreamVacati

Real travel guides. Free planning tools. Honest booking advice. Built by a traveler who got tired of vague, generic travel content.

Why DreamVacati Exists

I built DreamVacati because I couldn't find a single travel site that answered the question I actually had: "What's the realistic cost, best timing, and honest experience of going to [X]?" Most travel content is either vague inspiration or listicles full of affiliate links to things the author has never used.

DreamVacati is different. Every guide is written to give you a clear decision framework — not just "Greece is beautiful" but "here's the month-by-month breakdown of weather, prices, and crowds so you can pick the right time for your specific situation."

The free planning tools (budget calculator, packing list, pet travel estimator, requirements checker) exist because I needed them myself and couldn't find good versions anywhere. They're free to use, no signup required.
